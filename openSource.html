Godot Engine

The Godot Engine is an open-source video game engine, allowing users to develop both 2D and 3D games. To ensure the engine is as accessible as possible, the editor and documentation have been incrementally translated into numerous languages. As I have benefitted from open-source software and enjoyed video games created on open-source platforms, I seek to give back to the community by contributing in any way I can. As I am fluent in French, I regularly contribute by writing and editing strings in the French translation of Godot's properties, documentation, and editor.
